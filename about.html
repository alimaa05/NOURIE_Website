WHO ARE WE?

Created and founded by Honufah Hashim, NOURIE is a South-Asian owned business based in London, UK.

Our natural Cocosoy candles are handcrafted and carefully poured in small batches. We regularly test our products to make sure they are perfect and safe for you.



OUR STORY




As a Law student, I was stressed, struggling, and always found it difficult to find the time to take care of my wellbeing and have self-care moments, without feeling guilty for 'neglecting' my studies.

The rare nights when I wasn't in the library, it was face-masks, silk PJs and lighting candles that helped me to learn how to take the time out and embrace myself.

I created NOURIE with the beauty of simplicity, self-care, and self-love at the forefront.



OUR AIM




We want to help you to prioritise your self-care moments, by creating moments of calm and tranquillity.

Because you deserve the simple pleasures in life, no matter how busy it gets.


From our choice of natural wax to our use of eco-friendly and biodegradable packaging, we aim to minimise the harm caused to the environment, and to you as our consumer.

That's why NOURIE is a sustainable and earth-conscious business.



Indulge in moments of simplicity self-care and self-love with NOURIE. You deserve it!
