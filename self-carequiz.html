Self-Care Quiz

How kind are you to yourself? Do you unwind, relax and re-energise?

Complete our Self-Care Quiz and receive a Self-Care tip that is tailored to your needs!



1) I take time for myself everyday, even if it's only for 15 minutes.


2) I go for regular walks to clear my mind.


3) I have a minimum of 7-8 hours of sleep a night.


4) I write in a journal everyday or every other day.


5) I drink enough water to keep myself hydrated.


6) I have a balanced, nutritious diet.


7) I make time in my busy schedule to meet up with my friends outside of a work setting.


8) I mediatate at least 3 times a week.


9) I have a hobbie/engage in an activity other than work.

11) I have a relaxing bedtime routine.
